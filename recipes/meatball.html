Meatballs

Photo Credit: pexels.com @rachel-claire

Description

Meatball are a classic that are both quick and satisfying.
Serve with marinara on pasta, or even with white rice perhaps.
They also serve well with fries and gravy.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (1lb)
  • Egg (1, beaten)
  • Breadcrumbs (1/4cup)
  • Parmesan Cheese (1/4cup, grated)
  • Onion (1/4cup, chopped)
  • Garlic (2tsp, minced)
  • Kosher Salt (1tsp)
  • Black Pepper (1/2tsp, ground)
  • Fresh Parsley(2tbsp, chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. In large bowl combine all ingredients.
  3. Form the mixture into 1.5 inch balls and place on baking sheet.
  4. Bake 17-20 minutes,turning halfway, or until cooked through.
