Pancakes

Description

Pancakes, Believe it or now are a dinner hit in my house.
Serve with fresh fruit and/or bacon. Serve with maple syrup.
Enjoy this treat that the fam will rave about!

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour (1 1/2cups)
  • Baking Powder (3 1/2tsp)
  • White Sugar (1tbsp)
  • Salt (1/4tsp)
  • Milk (1 1/4cups)
  • Butter (3tbsp)
  • Egg (1, large)

Steps

  1. Heat lightly oiled pan or griddle at medium-high heat.
  2. In large bowl mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Mix in wet ingredients until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto pan using 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, about 2-3 minutes. Flip and repeat on other side.
