Tacos

Photo Credit: pexels.com @nano-erdozain

Description

A perfect dish for the family where everyone can get creative.
Can be made in about 20 minutes with all of your favorite ingredients.
This recipe uses ground beef and your preferred taco seasoning.
Fill them up with cheese, veggies of you choice, and a yummy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (1lb)
  • Taco Seasoning (1pkt)
  • Taco Shells (Soft)
  • Sour Cream
  • Grated Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Corn
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. In large sauce pan cook ground beef over medium-high heat until cooked through.
  2. Mix in 1/3 cup of water and sauce mix.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 4-5 minutes until thickened.
  4. Microwave taco shells for 30-45 seconds.
  5. Serve with desired toppings, I listed some recommendations in the ingredients above.
